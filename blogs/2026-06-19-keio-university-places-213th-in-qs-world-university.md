On June 18, 2026, Quacquarelli Symonds (QS) announced that Keio University placed 213th globally in the QS World University Rankings 2027, up from 215th the previous year, putting it in the top 14.1% of the 1,504 institutions evaluated. Keio ranked in the top 100 for Employer Reputation and Employment Outcomes.
